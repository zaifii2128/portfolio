A Makeup Artist

A makeup artist is a professional who transforms or enhances a person's appearance using cosmetics and specialized techniques. They work in various settings, including film, television, theater, fashion, and personal services, applying makeup to actors, models, clients, and others. Makeup artists need strong artistic skills, knowledge of color theory, and the ability to understand client needs and create desired looks.
